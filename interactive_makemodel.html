Distribution of Cars by Make, Model, Year

This interactive bar chart allows users to explore through the distribution of makes and the count of models in the state of Washington. This gives a more in depth view of which models are the most popular among people living in Washington, and gives more details on the distribution between different car models. Before looking more in depth into each car model, the most popular make in Washington is the Tesla Model Y. There are many car models that only have one make, such as VinFast, Think, and Rolls Royce. Many of these lower make and model counts are mainly based outside of the United States, so the representation of the cars within Washington may be much lower than globally.

This interactive bar chart allows users to explore through the distribution of models by model year. This shows the popularity of certain car models by each year, and display information about when companies enter the market. The first EV model year is 1999, with 2 Ford EVs being made then. Scrolling through the years, there are an increasing amount of cars as the model year increases, displaying the growth of popularity with EVs. The 2024 model year has the most variety of different companies and models.
